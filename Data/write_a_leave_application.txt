Here is a sample leave application:

**Leave Application**

**To:** [Manager's Name]
**Date:** [Current Date]

**Subject:** Request for Leave on [Leave Date(s)]

Dear [Manager's Name],

I am writing to request a leave of absence on [Leave Date(s)] as I need to [briefly mention the reason for leave, e.g. attend a family function, take care of a personal matter, etc.].

I will ensure that all my tasks are completed and my work is up to date before taking the leave. I will also make arrangements to ensure a smooth transition of my responsibilities during my absence.

The details of my leave are as follows:

* Leave Type: [Annual Leave, Sick Leave, etc.]
* Leave Dates: [Leave Date(s)]
* Number of Days: [Number of days]

I would greatly appreciate it if you could approve my request. If there are any concerns or issues, please let me know and I will do my best to address them.

Thank you for considering my request.

Sincerely,

[Your Name]
[Your Designation]
[Your Contact Information]

Please note that this is just a sample, and you should customize it according to your organization's policies and your specific needs.
